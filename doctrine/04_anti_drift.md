# Anti-drift

**Principle:** architectural inflation, refinement-loop drift, and self-referential conceptual elaboration are failure modes, not productivity.

**ELIF application:**
- New components cannot enter the architecture by being interesting; they enter only by surviving benchmark evidence
- The A/B/C/D classification system (see `procedure/abcd_classification_system.md`) is the structural defense against drift
- Doctrine documents are not expansion targets; they grow only when reality forces a new rule

**Primary defenses:** Rule 1 (Reality Confrontation Priority) and Rule 2 (Failure-Permitted Architecture). See `doctrine/02_locked_rules.md`.
